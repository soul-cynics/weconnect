What is We Connect?

We connect is similar to the shape and appearance of a flash drive. Its size is a bit bigger because of the features that the device has. It weighs 40g and has dimensions of 2.25 x 0.75 x 0.30 inches. The front view of the device shows the logo of We Connect and the power button. You can find the power button on the bottom center of the device. Our product comes with not one but two headphone jacks. The headphone jack has the standard size of a 3.5 mm that is located on the right side of the device. We Connect is made up of aluminum alloy with a hard-plastic coating. We used minimal design that comes in different colors.
