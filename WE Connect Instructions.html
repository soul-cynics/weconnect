INSTRUCTIONS

Using Headphone Jacks

1. Attach the device to your mobile phone.

2. Plug your headphones on the right edge of the device.

3. Play audio from the phone and enjoy.

Using Bluetooth Pairing

1. Attach the device to your mobile phone.

2. Press the power button on the We Connect device.

3. A permission request dialog will appear, toggle allow to grant access.

4. Go to Settings- Bluetooth.

5. Turn on Bluetooth and scan for devices.

6. Select the headphones to pair.

7. Play audio from the phone and enjoy.
